About Us

Built by students.
Secured for everyone.

Fun2Phish is a student-run IT team focused on building secure, reliable systems and spreading real cybersecurity awareness.

Fun2Phish started as a hands-on IT project with one goal: make cybersecurity education something people actually engage with. We believe the best defense against phishing is a workforce that knows what to look for.

This project includes a public-facing website and a secure employee intranet — a real-world simulation of how organizations structure and protect their digital infrastructure.

Every tool we build, every simulation we run, and every report we generate is designed with one thing in mind: turning awareness into action.
